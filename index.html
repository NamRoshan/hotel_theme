Accommodation

Suite Room

Our deluxe room comes with a king size bed. The interiors are designed keeping an ideal place for rest and restoration in mind, each room having temperature control options within. All rooms come with a stocked minibar, wifi connectivity and telephone service to make local and international phone calls.

Deluxe King Room

Room exude elegance and comfort including contemporary artwork with its one king bed, separate living and dining areas. The marble spacious bathroom features seperate bath tub and the vanity counter with amenities. Enjoy amenities such as daily local newspaper and wireless high speed internet access.
